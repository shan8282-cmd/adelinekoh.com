How I turned Claude into the APJ marketing team's most reliable teammate.

Five markets. One brand voice. No extra headcount. I bring the vision for AI to do the heavy lifting on production, so the team can spend its time on strategy, sales partnership, and the conversations that actually move pipeline.

The problem

Punching above our weight meant treating AI like our own ‘intern.’ A regional team of this size can't hand-craft a brief, a persona script, and a full set of market variants for every campaign, in every market, with the precision and speed the business needs. Content velocity was the bottleneck between strategy and revenue, not budget or headcount.

The system

I built an AI-enabled brief-to-publish workflow with Claude as the drafting engine — generating first-pass briefs and persona-specific scripting directly against our Salesforce data, so every asset started from the same source of truth instead of a blank page. Figma mapped the handoffs so a distributed team always knew what stage a piece of content was in and who owned the next step.

Why it's a GTM story, not just an AI story

The output wasn't "we use AI." It was more regional variants per sprint, shipped same-day, in a consistent brand voice — which is what actually lets a theatre GTM plan move at the speed the sales calendar demands. This is the model I'd bring to building AI-native GTM velocity for Cursor across APJ.
